In terms of our team game, the game will use“tower defense” to be the main basis, and players will need strategies and abilities to win the game. According to some biological knowledge, our game designs some attacks and defenses between inner body auto-immune system and some serious diseases.After that, we set up our main scenes including respiratory tract and lung, stomach, and blood vessel. In that way, the attacked objects could be a variety of diseases, and the defensive objects could be the immune organ, vaccine, medicine, and other medical supplies.

  • Integrating classic Tower Defense game with popular bioscience concepts .
  • Demand player to coordinate strategy and agility .
  • Witty and close-to-life storyline .
  • Collecting elements enable player to unlock various cells and virus profiles .

Our target audience are those who like strategy games or those who like agility games and want to know more about resistance mechanism o f human body. .
